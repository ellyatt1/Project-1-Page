GBDA 202: WYAWYG

Details of The Project:

The Task was to create posters that represented us individually as designers and show different aspects of our design interests. With this assignment I was given creative freedom to shape the posters visually.

My Inspiration for the poster themes and styles was based on a video game UI design from a title called Persona 5. I really like the comic book/pop design aesthetic and thought it would fit best for a poster about me. The Gen Ai used in this project was to edit the text bubbles I made as well as add the stars and POW graphic in the middle poster.
